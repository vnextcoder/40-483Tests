App bars take up zero none of the user's design surface until they're called up by the user (or by the developer). It's a handy way to provide unobtrusive user interaction. To call up the app bar, right click an empty area, hit WIN+Z, or swipe up from the bottom screen edge.
Most anything can go in an app bar. Even a video can be a button.
The app bar in this demo is entirely declarative. That's why you only see an HTML file.
